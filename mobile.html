Funeral Services

Traditional Funeral Services

Our traditional funeral services provide families with the opportunity to gather, remember, and celebrate the life of their loved one in a meaningful way. We understand that each family is unique, and we work closely with you to create a personalized service that reflects your loved one's personality, values, and wishes.

What's Included

  • Professional consultation and arrangement coordination
  • Care and preparation of your loved one
  • Use of our facilities for visitation and services
  • Coordination with clergy and cemetery
  • Transportation services within local area
  • Assistance with necessary permits and documentation
  • Memorial register book and acknowledgment cards

Personalization Options

We offer numerous ways to personalize your loved one's service, including memory displays, video tributes, special music selections, and custom memorial items. Our staff will guide you through all available options to create a truly meaningful celebration of life.

Professional Cremation Care

Our cremation services provide families with a dignified and respectful option while maintaining the same level of care and professionalism as traditional services. We offer flexible memorial options that can include visitation, services, and celebration of life gatherings.

Memorial Keepsakes

Pre-Need Arrangements

Planning Ahead

Pre-need planning allows you to make important decisions about your funeral arrangements in advance, ensuring your wishes are honored and relieving your family of difficult decisions during an emotional time. Our experienced staff will guide you through every step of the planning process.

Benefits of Pre-Planning

  • Ensure your personal wishes are honored
  • Relieve family members of difficult decisions
  • Lock in current prices and avoid future cost increases
  • Choose from all available service options at your own pace
  • Flexible payment plans available
  • Plans are transferable if you relocate
  • Pre-arrangements from other funeral homes are honored

Transfer Existing Arrangements

If you have pre-need arrangements made through another funeral home, we can help you transfer those arrangements to Jordan & Taylor Funeral and Cremation. Contact us to discuss the transfer process and ensure a smooth transition.

Value-Added Packages

Simplified Choice, Substantial Savings

Our value-added packages are designed to provide families with comprehensive funeral services at an affordable price. These packages offer the benefit of simplicity while ensuring all essential services are included, giving you peace of mind during a difficult time.

What's Included in Our Packages

Veteran Services

Honoring Those Who Served

Jordan & Taylor Funeral and Cremation is proud to serve our United States Veterans and their families. We understand the sacrifice made by those who served our country, and we are committed to providing the honor and respect they deserve.

Available Veteran Benefits

  • American flag presentation during services
  • Government-issued grave marker or plaque
  • Presidential Memorial Certificate
  • Burial space in National Cemetery for veteran and spouse
  • Military honors ceremony coordination
  • Assistance filing VA burial benefit claims
  • Help obtaining DD-214 discharge papers if needed

What You Need to Know

Please specify when making arrangements if your loved one was an active or honorably discharged veteran. We will guide you through the process of obtaining veteran benefits and coordinate military honors if desired. Please bring the veteran's DD-214 or discharge papers to the arrangement meeting. If you don't have these documents, we can assist in obtaining them.

Complete Arrangements

Full-Service Coordination

Our complete arrangement services handle every detail of your loved one's services, from initial planning through final arrangements. We coordinate all aspects of the funeral or memorial service, allowing you to focus on family and remembering your loved one.

Comprehensive Services Include

  • Coordination with clergy and religious organizations
  • Personalized obituary creation and editing
  • Publication in local and out-of-state newspapers
  • Online memorial and tribute page
  • Assistance with Social Security and VA notifications
  • Help filing insurance claims and paperwork
  • Coordination with cemetery or crematory
  • Arrangement of flowers and memorial donations
  • Organization of reception or gathering
  • Follow-up grief support resources

Dedicated Support

Your funeral director will serve as your single point of contact throughout the entire process, ensuring seamless coordination and communication. We handle all the details so you don't have to, providing support and guidance every step of the way.

Payment Options & Guidelines

We offer flexible payment options to accommodate your needs

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Cash
  • Cashier's Check
  • Visa, Mastercard, Discover or American Express
  • Verifiable Irrevocable Insurance Assignment
  • Pre-Paid Advance Planning
  • Advance Payment by Receiving Funeral Home
  • Advance Payment by Forwarding Funeral Home
*There is a convenience fee of 4% for insurance processing via Tribute Insurance Assignment.

Important Guidelines

  • Payments must be made 48 hours in advance of services
  • All services are pending until paid or approved for payment
  • Insurance policies MUST be presented before final arrangements
  • Cannot accept assignment on policies within contestable period (under 24 months)
  • May deny assignment on policies payable to an estate
  • Embalming and casket required for public viewing
Provisions for payment are due at the time of arrangement.
